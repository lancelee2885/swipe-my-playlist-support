## FAQ

### How does Swipe My Playlist work?
Pick a playlist from your Apple Music library, then swipe through each song. Swipe right to keep, left to remove. When you're done, tap "Create Cleaned Playlist" and a new playlist with only your kept songs is added to your library.

### Does it delete songs from my original playlist?
No. Your original playlist is never modified. A brand new playlist is created with the songs you chose to keep.

### What does the new playlist get named?
By default it's named "{Your Playlist} (Cleaned)", but you can edit the name before creating it.

### Why does the app need Apple Music access?
The app reads your playlists and songs so you can swipe through them, and creates a new playlist when you confirm. It cannot access any data beyond your music library.

## Privacy Policy

Swipe My Playlist respects your privacy.

- **Music data** is accessed only through Apple's MusicKit framework and is used solely to display your playlists and songs within the app. We do not collect, store, or transmit your music library data to any server.
- **Advertising:** The app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). You can remove ads with a one-time in-app purchase.
- **No accounts:** The app does not require you to create an account or sign in.
- **No analytics:** We do not use any third-party analytics services beyond what AdMob provides.
- **Data storage:** All app preferences (such as shuffle mode and theme) are stored locally on your device.

If you have any privacy-related questions, contact us at [lancelee2885@gmail.com](mailto:lancelee2885@gmail.com).
